Action is at 0x2C.
animIndex is more informative for the purposes of a ghost,
but this information can be useful too.

00 - Teleporting in/dying/exploding
01 - bottomless pit death
02 - knockback
03 - standing still
04 - tiptoe
05 - running
06 - jumping
07 - skidding to a stop
08 - landing
09 - Climbing ladder
0A - Ladder top (ass)
0B - teleporting out
